Black Magic Reference - মারকাযুর রুকইয়াহ


Question: What does islam say about black magic and taveez do we have to belive in such things does it exits one who has faith in allah that no one but allah does everything still it will effect that person plss clarify as i have seen many imam giving taveez and i am confused also as we belive in one allah he does everything and also one imam saying that black magic was also done to aap muhammed salalaahou alaihie wasalam(pbuh).

 

Answer:

Bismillah hir-Rahman nir-Rahim !

(Fatwa: 1525/253=L/1429)

قال الشامي: وفي شرح الزعفراني: السحر حق عندنا وجوده وتصوره و أثره (شامي: 1/143)

It means that we deem right the existence of magic, its imagination and its effect. Magic can leave its effect on any person, the Prophet (صلى الله عليه وسلم) himself was affected by magic done by the daughters of Labid. There are some Quranic verses and duas if recited punctually there will no effect of magic, in-sha-Allah.

 

Hadhrat Kaab Al-Ahbar (رضى الله عنه) says had he not recited some words, the Jews would have made (through magic) him a donkey, someone asked him what the words are, he said:

أعوذ بوجه العظيم الذي ليس شيئ أعظم منه وبكلمات الله التامات التي لايجاوزهن بر ولا فاجر وبأسماء الله الحسنى ما علمت منها وما لم أعلم من شر ما خلق وزرا وبرا

Allah will protect those reciting this dua at least thrice in the morning and evening.

 

Also, Hadhrat Shaikhul Hadith (Maulana Zakariya) selected nearly 32 Quranic verses in a collection named 'Manzil', by reciting it once in the morning and evening one will be protected from the effect of magic, in-sha-Allah.

Allah (Subhana Wa Ta'ala) knows Best

Darul Ifta,

Darul Uloom Deoband, India
